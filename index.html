About the founder

Aikord started from a simple frustration: most AI products promise too much, fail too often, and are not built with the discipline real users need.

Built by Apoorv Ahluwalia

Aikord is the founder-led vision of Apoorv Ahluwalia, a software engineer from Gurugram, India, building with a deep bias toward strong systems, practical products, and long-term quality.

The vision behind Aikord is to build AI products that people can actually rely on—products that combine modern intelligence with disciplined engineering, clear product thinking, and a genuine focus on outcomes.

Instead of chasing trends, Aikord is being shaped as a studio for thoughtful AI products in domains where trust, execution, and structure matter.
